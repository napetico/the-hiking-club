We hike, we mingle, we have fun.

We're a bunch of nature enthusiast and outdoors sports lovers that get together every weekend for a refreshing hike in good company.

Our plans are always 1-day group adventures to beautiful locations near our city, with moderate levels of difficulty and fun extra-activities in each route (the majority of which are kids and pets friendly).

Know more about our club >>

Stay on track, stay safe, keep the good vibes.

We want to make our hikes the highlight of the week for everyone that comes. That's why we try to keep a relaxed but well-organized family-trip vibe.

Read details, ground-rules, and recommendations for each trip on the Upcoming section. Make sure to be on time at the meeting point, share car when possible, and bring your food, water, and equipment.

During our hikes we can relax, exercise, play, make new friends, or what ever fills your heart. Walk at your own pace, but stay close and don't leave the track. Follow our guides indications, pick up after your own trash, and have fun.
